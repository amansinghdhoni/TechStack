Did You Know?

🌊 Over 70% of Earth’s surface is covered by oceans.
🐙 The deepest part of the ocean is about 11,000 meters deep (Mariana Trench).
🐠 Coral reefs support more species than any other marine environment.
🌡 Oceans absorb about 30% of the CO₂ released by humans.
🧪 Ocean water contains about 3.5% salt.
💨 Ocean currents move more water than all rivers combined.
